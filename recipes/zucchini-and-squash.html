Skillet Zucchini and Squash

Description

Yellow squash, zucchini, onions, and red bell peppers are abundant in the summer, and they make a beautiful presentation in this easy side dish. Garnish with a few fresh chives, if you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 0.5 medium onion, chopped
  • 0.5 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium zucchini, cut into 0.25-inch slices
  • 1 medium yellow squash, cut into 0.25-inch slices
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • minced chives (optional)

Directions

  1. Heat butter and olive oil together in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
    Add onion once butter is bubbly; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add bell and pepper and cook for 1 minute.
    Stir in yellow squash and zucchini slices. Season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, for about 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in minced garlic and cook, stirring, about 1 minute.
    Keep cooking, stirring continuously, until squash has desired texture, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle with minced chives before serving.
